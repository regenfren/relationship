Being a Relational Manager

Relationship-Oriented Leadership

What is Relationship-Oriented Leadership?

Relationship-oriented leadership, also known as people-oriented leadership, focuses on supporting and motivating teams or work groups and the relationships therein. Relationship-oriented leaders prioritize healthy work environments, team cohesion, and group members' individual and collective satisfaction.

Relationship-oriented leaders are more democratic and often utilize group decision-making methods.
